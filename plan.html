Plan your Trip

Your journey to discovery begins here.

Nestled on the western coast of Panay Island, Antique is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Known for its unspoiled natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm, welcoming communities, the province offers a travel experience unlike any other. From the crystal-clear waters of its islands and the soothing flow of its rivers, to the majestic peaks of its mountains and the stories preserved in its historic landmarks, Antique invites you to slow down, explore deeper, and connect with both nature and culture.

Planning your trip is the first step toward discovering everything this province has to offer. Whether you’re dreaming of island hopping under the tropical sun, trekking through lush forests, tasting authentic local cuisine, or simply relaxing by the beach, Antique provides countless opportunities to create meaningful and memorable experiences. Every journey here is shaped by your interests—whether you seek adventure, leisure, cultural immersion, or a little bit of everything.

This guide is designed to help you plan with ease and confidence. Learn how to get to Antique, find the best places to stay, explore transportation options, and discover helpful travel tips to make your trip smooth and enjoyable. You’ll also find suggested itineraries and insights to help you make the most of your time, whether you’re visiting for a quick escape or an extended vacation.

Let Antique inspire your next adventure—where every destination tells a story, every experience feels authentic, and every moment becomes a memory worth keeping.
