Highlights and Activities

📍Top Activities To Do

Antique is not just a destination; it’s a living adventure. From cascading waterfalls to peaceful islands, from adrenaline-filled hikes to serene relaxation, every experience here immerses you in nature, culture, and local tradition. Whether you’re a thrill-seeker, a nature lover, or someone looking for a peaceful escape, Antique has something for everyone.

ADVENTURE & OUTDOOR FUN

For those who love a rush of adrenaline, Antique offers unique outdoor activities:

River Tubing in Tibiao

Waterfall Trekking

Mountain Hiking
  • Glide through calm and winding rivers surrounded by lush forest and mangroves.
  • Suitable for beginners and families, but the occasional gentle rapids give a fun thrill.
  • Local guides provide safety gear and instructions—no experience needed.
  • Explore hidden waterfalls deep within Antique’s tropical forests.
  • Trails often include swinging rope bridges, natural rock pools, and scenic photo spots.
  • Bring waterproof shoes and a change of clothes—you’ll likely want to swim in the refreshing pools!
  • Trek trails like Mount Madja-as for panoramic views of Antique’s mountains, rivers, and coastline.
  • Watch for endemic birds, rare orchids, and other wildlife along the way.
  • Sunrise hikes are especially magical—catch misty peaks and the golden glow over rice terraces.

💡 Insider Tip: Early morning is the best time for hiking and trekking—it’s cooler, quieter, and you can spot wildlife.




WATER AND BEACH ACTIVITIES

Antique is blessed with pristine beaches, clear rivers, and hidden coves—perfect for water adventures:

Swimming & Sunbathing

Snorkeling & Island Hopping

Cliff Diving & Natural Pools
  • Relax on white sand beaches on Malalison Island or secluded coastal spots.
  • Calm waters are safe for swimming and wading—great for families or solo travelers.
  • Explore vibrant coral reefs around small islands and discover colorful tropical fish.
  • Many local fishermen offer boat tours and snorkeling gear for a full day adventure.
  • Brave cliffs into refreshing pools at select waterfalls or coastal spots.
  • Perfect for thrill-seekers, but always check depth and safety first.

💡 Insider Tip: Pack a waterproof bag to carry your phone or camera—you’ll want to capture these moments.




RELAXATION AND WELLNESS

Antique is blessed with pristine beaches, clear rivers, and hidden coves—perfect for water adventures:

Kawa Hot Bath in Tibiao

Sunset Watching

Peaceful Island Retreats
  • Soak in a giant metal cauldron heated over wood fire, surrounded by forest and river sounds.
  • Perfect for relaxing sore muscles after hiking or river adventures.
  • Evening baths under the stars are magical and calming.
  • Find a quiet beach or hilltop to watch breathtaking sunsets.
  • Ideal for photography, meditation, or simply enjoying nature’s beauty.
  • Many smaller islands remain largely untouched by tourism, offering serene, uncrowded beaches.
  • Spend a day reading, snorkeling, or kayaking in complete tranquility.

💡 Insider Tip: Bring a lightweight hammock for beachside naps—nothing beats swinging gently while listening to the waves.
