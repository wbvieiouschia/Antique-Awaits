Safety Tips for Traveling

Travel smart, stay safe, and enjoy your journey in Antique to the fullest.

Exploring Antique is an exciting adventure, but like any destination, it’s important to prioritize safety so your trip is smooth and worry-free. Here are essential tips for travelers:

🛡️ General Travel

  • Keep valuables secure and never leave bags unattended.
  • Carry photocopies of your ID and important documents.
  • Inform someone of your daily plans when trekking.

⛰️ Outdoor & Adventure

  • Wear appropriate gear (life jackets, sturdy shoes).
  • Check weather conditions before climbs or boat trips.
  • Stay hydrated and always apply sunscreen.

🚐 Transportation

  • Use reputable bus or van services between towns.
  • Always wear a helmet if renting motorbikes.
  • Avoid traveling alone late at night in unfamiliar areas.

🏥 Health & Hygiene

  • Drink bottled/purified water; avoid tap water.
  • Carry insect repellent for forest and river visits.
  • Bring personal medications for allergies or motion sickness.

🌱 Respect & Environment

  • Follow local customs at heritage sites and villages.
  • Avoid littering; use eco-friendly practices.
  • Respect rules in protected waterfalls and forests.
