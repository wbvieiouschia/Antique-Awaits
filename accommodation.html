Accomodation

Offers accommodations that suit every traveler’s style and budget.

Antique has a wide range of accommodations that allow visitors to experience the province comfortably while staying close to nature, culture, and adventure. Whether you prefer the luxury of a beachfront resort, the rustic charm of an eco-lodge, or the warmth of a local homestay, there’s something for everyone.

1. Luxury & Premium Stays 🌴

Perfect for travelers looking for comfort, ocean views, and resort-style amenities.

📍 Options: Mararison Island, Pandan Bay resorts

✨ Amenities: Pools, beachfront access, spa treatments

Plan Idea

2 Nights: Day 1 – Sunset dinner; Day 2 – Island hopping; Day 3 – Departure.

2. Eco & Nature Retreats 🌿

A peaceful escape surrounded by mountains, rivers, or forests.

📍 Options: Mt. Madja-as, Sibalom forests, Tibiao rivers

✨ Activities: Hiking, birdwatching, river tubing

Plan Idea

3-Day Adventure: Trekking, river exploration, and morning meditation.

3. Cultural & Homestay Plans 🏡

Experience the heart of Antique’s local life and traditions.

📍 Options: San Jose, Libertad, or Hamtic villages

✨ Experience: Cooking workshops, farm visits, village tours

Plan Idea

2-Day Immersion: Traditional cooking and local craft lessons.

4. Budget & Guesthouse 🛏️

For backpackers who prefer simple and affordable accommodations.

📍 Options: Inns in San Jose, Pandan, or Culasi

✨ Best For: Town exploration and local food tasting

Plan Idea

3-4 Day Backpacking: Town hopping, rivers, and nearby beaches.

Tips for Choosing 💡

✅ Match stay to activities
📅 Book ahead for festivals
🔄 Combine different stays
🤝 Support local businesses
