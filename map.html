How to get there?

“Reaching Antique is part of the adventure—scenic, convenient, and worth the journey.”

Traveling to Antique is easy and offers a glimpse of the beautiful landscapes of Panay Island along the way.

By Air ✈️

Fly into Iloilo International Airport. Then, take a bus or van for a 2-3 hour scenic journey to Antique.

By Land 🚌

Buses and vans travel regularly from nearby provinces on Panay Island:

  • Buses: Daily from Iloilo City to San Jose.
  • Vans: Faster service to Culasi, Pandan, or Tibiao.

By Sea ⛴️

Ferries provide access to coastal towns—a relaxing way to arrive during island-hopping trips.

💡

Travel Tip

Travel during daylight hours to enjoy the views and ensure easy access to local transport.
