Getting Around Antique

Explore Antique with ease—whether by land, sea, or air, getting around is simple and scenic.

While Antique is a laid-back province, it’s well-connected internally and to neighboring islands and cities. Choosing the right mode of transport can make your trip smooth, comfortable, and more enjoyable.

🚌 Main Bus Routes (to & within Antique)

1. Iloilo City → San Jose de Buenavista (Capital)

📍 Start: Mohon Terminal (Iloilo)

📍 End: San Jose, Antique

🚍 Bus: Ceres / Vans

Route: Oton → Tigbauan → Miagao → San Joaquin → Antique towns → San Jose

👉 Main gateway to Antique. Travel time: 3–4 hours.

2. Iloilo City → Culasi / Pandan (North)

📍 Start: Mohon Terminal

📍 End: Culasi / Pandan

Route: San Jose → Tibiao → Culasi → Pandan

👉 Used for: Malalison Island and Northern beaches.

🚐 Jeepney & Local Routes

Route Type / Destination
Iloilo → Antique Molo Terminal → San Jose / Anini-y (Budget choice)
Mohon → Culasi Modern Jeepney route connecting to the North
Town-to-Town San Jose ↔ Sibalom | Culasi ↔ Tibiao | Pandan ↔ Libertad
