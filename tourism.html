Antique - The Hidden Gem

💳 Tourism

The entire coastline of Antique is ideal for scuba diving, with clear waters and diverse marine life attracting both beginners and experienced divers. Starting from the southernmost towns, tourists can visit Sira-an Hot Spring, which is believed to have therapeutic and medical properties, making it a relaxing stop for visitors seeking wellness experiences.

Moving northward, travelers reach San Jose de Buenavista, the provincial capital. Along the way, they can stop at the Malandog Marker, commemorating the site of the first Malayan settlement on Panay Island, and take in the sights of the newly constructed Marina Bay, a hub for leisure and water-based activities. San Jose also offers modern tourism amenities, including restaurants serving local and international cuisine, two shopping malls, pasalubong centers, hotels, and beach resorts that cater to a variety of budgets.

For nature enthusiasts, Sibalom Natural Park is a must-visit. It is home to the Rafflesia, the largest flower in the world, as well as a rich array of wildlife, hiking trails, and scenic viewpoints. Visitors can also explore traditional agricultural practices in the municipalities of Patnongon and Laua-an, where muscovado sugar mills still operate using time-honored methods. In Laua-an, tourists can sample and purchase butong-butong, a sweet delicacy made from muscovado sugar, and other local products.

In Tibiao, adventure and wellness activities abound. Visitors can experience the famous kawa bath, a giant cauldron-style herbal bath, hike to Bugtong Bato Falls, or enjoy a unique Fish SPA, where tiny fish gently nibble on dead skin for a relaxing treatment. From the town of Culasi, travelers can view Mount Madja-as, often called the "Mount Olympus" of Antique, which offers challenging hikes and breathtaking scenery.

Sebaste is another tourism highlight, boasting the Igpasungaw Falls, Sebaste Inland Resort, and the historically significant Saint Blaise Church, which attracts thousands of pilgrims annually during its fiesta. Meanwhile, Pandan is famous for the Malumpati Health Spring and Bugang River, recognized as one of the cleanest rivers in the Philippines. Here, tourists can enjoy river rafting, boating, and nature excursions, with organized tour packages available to guide the experience.

In the northern municipalities, Libertad is renowned for traditional handicrafts such as bariw mats and woven bags, allowing visitors to purchase authentic local products. San Remigio, recently dubbed the "summer capital" of Antique, offers cool mountain air, scenic views, and even a strawberry farm, making it a favorite spot for families and tourists looking to escape the heat.

From coastlines to mountains, hot springs to waterfalls, Antique offers a rich mix of adventure, culture, and relaxation, making it a diverse and growing destination for both local and international tourists.
